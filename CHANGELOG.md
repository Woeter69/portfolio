# Changelog

All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

## [Unreleased] - 2026-07-05

### Added
- **Interactive Terminal Command System**: Overhauled `TypewriterTerminal.tsx` to feature a functional terminal interface supporting commands (`help`, `about`, `skills`, `projects`, `contact`, `clear`, `exit`) when the user interacts with the typewriter.
- **Mechanical Typewriter Physics & Animations**: Added tactile keystroke animations including physical key depression, realistic key tilt, striking typebar animation, and carriage return acoustics/visuals.
- **Two-Stage Cinematic Sitting Animation**: Implemented a smooth two-stage camera transition in `ScrollWrapper.tsx` that glides the player to a standing position in front of the chair before lowering them down into a seated typing position.
- **Warm Typography & Lighting**: Upgraded typewriter interface font to Google Fonts `Outfit`, added a warm glowing cursor, and integrated ambient desk illumination.
- **Hold-to-Crouch Stealth Movement**: Implemented smooth crouching physics in `PlayerController.tsx`. Holding `C` lowers player eye level from `-42.1` down to `-42.6` with reduced stealth movement speed (`35.0`), smoothly standing back up upon releasing the key.

### Fixed
- **Camera Facing Direction at Typewriter**: Corrected camera orientation when seated at the typewriter desk in `ScrollWrapper.tsx`. The camera now sits in the chair position (`chairStandPos`: `[1.85, -42.1, -23.65]`, `typingSeatPos`: `[1.85, -42.52, -23.28]`) and directs its look-at vector onto the desk (`targetLook`: `[1.85, -42.00, -23.85]`), calibrated to lower the gaze from the ceiling/sky directly onto the typewriter keyboard and paper.
- **Typewriter Exit Collision Fix**: Resolved an issue in `ScrollWrapper.tsx` where exiting the typewriter left the player's camera trapped inside the desk AABB collision box (`Z = -23.28`), preventing normal walking movement. Upon exiting, the player now stands up cleanly outside the desk boundary at `[1.85, -42.1, -23.65]`, preserving normal walking mechanics and movement.
- **Kitchen Counter Drawer & Oven Clipping**: Shifted the built-in oven, stovetop base, and burners further to the right (`Z = -0.45`) along the kitchen counter in `CabinInterior.tsx`, and realigned the drawer fronts and lower cabinets (`[-1.00, 0.10, 0.85]`) with custom widths to cleanly frame the oven without intersecting or clipping into the oven window or handles.
- **Typewriter Sitting Animation Stability & Intuitive Exit**: Resolved severe screen shaking and chair entrapment when interacting with the typewriter. In `PlayerController.tsx`, physics execution and pointer lock captures are now cleanly suspended during terminal focus, stopping AABB boundary collisions from fighting camera transitions. In `ScrollWrapper.tsx`, simplified the two-stage sitting curve to remove double-lerping wobble. Added intuitive outside-click detection in `TypewriterTerminal.tsx` so clicking anywhere outside the typewriter model immediately stands the player up out of the chair without getting stuck.
- **Typewriter Keyboard Visibility & Number Row Addition**: Shifted the main lower chassis box of the typewriter back to end at `Z = +0.06` and extended the front keyboard slant tray forward to `Z = +0.28` in `TypewriterTerminal.tsx`. This prevents the top row (`QWERTYUIOP`) from clipping inside the main chassis body. Also introduced a full mechanical Number Row (`1234567890`) above QWERTY with responsive key depression and tilt physics.
- **Detective Conspiracy Board**: Transformed the simple cabin pinboard in `CabinInterior.tsx` into an elaborate, movie-style Detective Conspiracy Board. Added 8 distinct evidence items including Polaroid suspect photos, Project Proteus blueprints, confidential Top Secret folders, Sector 4 topographic target maps, news articles, lab fingerprint cards, and sticky notes—each anchored with colored pushpins. Connected the entire board with 12 criss-crossing 3D CatmullRom red and gold threads featuring realistic downward gravity sag.
- **Kitchen Oven Window Geometry Fix**: Fixed an issue in `CabinInterior.tsx` where the dark glass oven window plane was missing a Y-axis rotation (`rotation={[0, -Math.PI / 2, 0]}`), causing it to stand perpendicular to the oven face and stick out sideways into the room walkway. Aligned the window flush against the oven door and added silver handle mounts.
- **Detective Board Flush Wall Attachment (`Z = 2.45`) & Visible Evidence Pictures**: Pushed the Detective Conspiracy Board all the way back to `Z = 2.45` so its back attaches flush against the inner surface of the back wall (`Z = 2.46`), completely removing any air gap. Restored and properly layered all 8 distinct evidence items (Polaroid photos, Project Proteus blueprints, Secret folders, topographic sector maps, newspaper articles, sticky notes, and forensic lab cards) on the corkboard surface (`local Z = 0.025`) so each picture is clearly visible along with pushpins and criss-crossing 3D Catmull-Rom red threads.
- **Vintage Two-Way / Ham Radio Station**: Placed a detailed retro Firewatch Two-Way / Ham Radio Station on the clean kitchen countertop along the right wall (`X = 1.95, Y = y + 0.87, Z = -1.38`) to the left of the stovetop/oven in `CabinInterior.tsx` overlooking the room, featuring an olive-green metal chassis, speaker grille, glowing amber dial, tuning knobs, handset microphone, and a long steel telescopic antenna.
- **Interactive Multi-Station Radio & Web Audio Synth Engine**: Created a dedicated `VintageRadio.tsx` component that allows users to click the radio to switch between 3 procedural Web Audio API stations (`98.4 FM - LOFI CHILL BEATS`, `104.2 FM - FOREST WATCH AMBIENCE`, and `107.9 FM - RETRO SYNTH CHILL`) or turn it OFF. Added tactile mechanical click sounds on dial turn, dynamic canvas HUD station readouts, glowing pulsing dials, rotating tuning knobs, and floating 3D music note particles drifting out of the speaker grille.
- **Typewriter & Radio Interactive Floating Popups & Text Overflow Fix**: Added a floating 3D canvas readout popup above the Typewriter (`TypewriterTerminal.tsx`) showing active/inactive instructions (`⌨️ TYPEWRITER TERMINAL — CLICK TO TYPE COMMANDS`), and fixed text overflow clipping in both the Radio and Typewriter popups by increasing canvas resolution (`768x144`), widening the readout mesh geometry, and using a clean, readable bold sans-serif system font.
- **Interactive Toggleable Table Lamp**: Created a dedicated `DeskLamp.tsx` component replacing the static desk lamp in `CabinInterior.tsx`. Users can click the lamp to toggle its warm golden point light and emissive bulb glow ON/OFF, accompanied by a realistic Web Audio pull-chain click sound and a floating 3D readout badge (`💡 DESK LAMP [ON] — CLICK TO TURN OFF`).
- **Authentic Typewriter Audio, Firewatch Log Header & Smart Page Ripping**: Replaced generic terminal branding in `TypewriterTerminal.tsx` with authentic `FIREWATCH STATION #04 LOG`, added procedural Web Audio API sounds for every keystroke (snappy mechanical metal strike clack), carriage return (classic bell ring + slide), and page ejection (crisp paper tear). Implemented smart page-ripping logic: if a typed command and its output would exceed physical sheet limits (`>= 11 lines`), the current page rips off cinematically and the new command output prints cleanly at the top of a fresh sheet of paper.
- **Hide Typewriter Popup While Typing**: Updated `TypewriterTerminal.tsx` so the floating readout popup (`⌨️ TYPEWRITER TERMINAL — CLICK TO TYPE COMMANDS`) is only shown when hovering over the idle typewriter and completely disappears when actively typing (`isActive`), keeping the paper view unobstructed.
- **Seamless Escape from Typewriter back to First-Person Explore Mode**: Fixed an issue in `PlayerController.tsx` where pressing `Escape` while focused on the typewriter would exit Explore Mode entirely. Now, when leaving the typewriter (`focusedProp === 'none'`), `isExploreMode` stays active and pointer lock automatically relocks so you can instantly resume moving and looking around the watchtower without clicking again.
- **Authentic Industrial Ceiling Pendant Shade & Roof Light Bleed Fix**: Replaced the floating point light source near the cabin ceiling in `CabinInterior.tsx` with a detailed `CeilingPendantLight` fixture featuring a metal ceiling flange, conduit rod, enamel industrial dome shade, and glowing warm Edison bulb. Bounded the point light distance (`distance={3.8}`) so that warm interior illumination stays inside the cabin and never bleeds through the ceiling onto the outside roof of the tower.
- **Detective Conspiracy Board Relocated to Bed Side Wall Center**: Moved the Detective Conspiracy Board from the back wall to the bed side left wall (`X = -2.45, Y = y + 1.8, Z = 0.0`) with `rotation={[0, -Math.PI / 2, 0]}` so it sits exactly in the middle along the wall and faces directly into the room.
- **Detective Board Anti-Flicker & Z-Fighting Fix**: Fixed an issue where the Detective Conspiracy Board would blink or glitch when walking by pulling the board center away from the wall plane (`X = -2.38`) to prevent wall clipping, and establishing strict non-overlapping Z-depth separation across every corkboard backing, wood frame, paper evidence card, photo layer, red thread, and pushpin.
- **Detective Board Inner Border Glitch Fix (4-Piece Architectural Frame)**: Replaced stacked overlapping corkboard and frame box geometries with a clean 4-piece architectural wood frame (Top, Bottom, Left, and Right rails) surrounding a single dedicated corkboard panel. Eliminates all shared coplanar inner border faces so the border never glitches or z-fights.
- **Snap Detective Board Flush to Left Wall**: Snapped the Detective Board position to `X = -2.45` so the back surface of the 4-piece wood frame touches the left wall inner surface (`X = -2.46`) exactly flush without any floating air gap.
