# Changelog

All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

## [Unreleased] - 2026-07-05

### Added
- **Interactive Terminal Command System**: Overhauled `TypewriterTerminal.tsx` to feature a functional terminal interface supporting commands (`help`, `about`, `skills`, `projects`, `contact`, `clear`, `exit`) when the user interacts with the typewriter.
- **Mechanical Typewriter Physics & Animations**: Added tactile keystroke animations including physical key depression, realistic key tilt, striking typebar animation, and carriage return acoustics/visuals.
- **Two-Stage Cinematic Sitting Animation**: Implemented a smooth two-stage camera transition in `ScrollWrapper.tsx` that glides the player to a standing position in front of the chair before lowering them down into a seated typing position.
- **Warm Typography & Lighting**: Upgraded typewriter interface font to Google Fonts `Outfit`, added a warm glowing cursor, and integrated ambient desk illumination.
- **Hold-to-Crouch Stealth Movement**: Implemented smooth crouching physics in `PlayerController.tsx`. Holding `C` lowers player eye level from `-42.1` down to `-42.6` with reduced stealth movement speed (`35.0`), smoothly standing back up upon releasing the key.

### Fixed
- **Camera Facing Direction at Typewriter**: Corrected camera orientation when seated at the typewriter desk in `ScrollWrapper.tsx`. The camera now sits in the chair position (`chairStandPos`: `[1.85, -42.1, -23.65]`, `typingSeatPos`: `[1.85, -42.52, -23.28]`) and directs its look-at vector onto the desk (`targetLook`: `[1.85, -42.00, -23.85]`), calibrated to lower the gaze from the ceiling/sky directly onto the typewriter keyboard and paper.
- **Typewriter Exit Collision Fix**: Resolved an issue in `ScrollWrapper.tsx` where exiting the typewriter left the player's camera trapped inside the desk AABB collision box (`Z = -23.28`), preventing normal walking movement. Upon exiting, the player now stands up cleanly outside the desk boundary at `[1.85, -42.1, -23.65]`, preserving normal walking mechanics and movement.
- **Kitchen Counter Drawer & Oven Clipping**: Shifted the built-in oven, stovetop base, and burners further to the right (`Z = -0.45`) along the kitchen counter in `CabinInterior.tsx`, and realigned the drawer fronts and lower cabinets (`[-1.00, 0.10, 0.85]`) with custom widths to cleanly frame the oven without intersecting or clipping into the oven window or handles.
- **Typewriter Sitting Animation Stability & Intuitive Exit**: Resolved severe screen shaking and chair entrapment when interacting with the typewriter. In `PlayerController.tsx`, physics execution and pointer lock captures are now cleanly suspended during terminal focus, stopping AABB boundary collisions from fighting camera transitions. In `ScrollWrapper.tsx`, simplified the two-stage sitting curve to remove double-lerping wobble. Added intuitive outside-click detection in `TypewriterTerminal.tsx` so clicking anywhere outside the typewriter model immediately stands the player up out of the chair without getting stuck.
- **Typewriter Keyboard Visibility & Number Row Addition**: Shifted the main lower chassis box of the typewriter back to end at `Z = +0.06` and extended the front keyboard slant tray forward to `Z = +0.28` in `TypewriterTerminal.tsx`. This prevents the top row (`QWERTYUIOP`) from clipping inside the main chassis body. Also introduced a full mechanical Number Row (`1234567890`) above QWERTY with responsive key depression and tilt physics.
- **Detective Conspiracy Board**: Transformed the simple cabin pinboard in `CabinInterior.tsx` into an elaborate, movie-style Detective Conspiracy Board. Added 8 distinct evidence items including Polaroid suspect photos, Project Proteus blueprints, confidential Top Secret folders, Sector 4 topographic target maps, news articles, lab fingerprint cards, and sticky notes—each anchored with colored pushpins. Connected the entire board with 12 criss-crossing 3D CatmullRom red and gold threads featuring realistic downward gravity sag.
